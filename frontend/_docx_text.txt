Thermal Printing Paper Roll


Thermal printing paper roll is a specialized paper with a thermal coating on the surface. It displays images and text through a thermal reaction, offering advantages such as inkless printing, fast response, environmental protection, ease of use, and durability. This makes it ideal for efficient working environments. High-quality thermal paper provides clear, durable prints and is suitable for multiple industries.


Advantages and Features


Inkless Printing: No need for ink or ribbons, reducing maintenance and operational costs.


Fast Response: Quick printing speeds for high-efficiency environments.


Environmental Protection: BPA-free and meets modern safety and environmental standards.


Ease of Use: Simple and convenient for various applications.


Durability: Produces long-lasting, clear prints.

Carbonless paper

Carbonless Paper is a multi-layer copying paper that does not require the use of carbon paper. It is also called carbonless copy paper or NCR paper. It is often used in situations where multiple copies are required such as invoices, receipts, orders and contracts. Compared with traditional carbon paper, 
Ncr
 carbonless 
paperis
 more environmentally friendly, reduces the waste of carbon paper, and is easier to recycle. Because of its ability to directly generate copies, it is less likely to produce redundant or accidental copies, ensuring the confidentiality of information and saving time. and reduce errors.

With the continuous development of carbonless paper rolls technology, its application scenarios are gradually expanding. In addition to traditional business paperwork, more and more companies are using carbonless multi part paper to generate bills, logistics documents and so on. The popularity of modern office equipment and printing technology also makes the use of carbonless duplicate paper more convenient and efficient.

Advantages of carbonless paper wholesale

✅ No toner required, clean and environmentally friendly – ​​Get rid of the toner pollution of traditional carbon paper, cleaner and more environmentally friendly.

✅ Multi-copy printing, efficient and convenient – ​​Can be made into 2 copies (CB+CF), 3 copies (CB+CFB+CF) or more copies to meet different business needs.

✅ Clear writing, long-lasting and non-fading – Special microcapsule coating technology ensures clear and durable handwriting.

✅ Widely applicable and highly compatible – Suitable for dot matrix printers and handwritten filling, commonly used in logistics, banks, hospitals, finance, factories and other fields.

✅ Support customization to meet individual needs – Provide customized size, color (white, pink, yellow, blue, green), printing LOGO and other services to enhance corporate image.
